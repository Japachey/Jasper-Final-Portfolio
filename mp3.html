Major Project 3: Phishing Awareness Toolkit

Designing safer communication for Georgia Tech students

Project Overview

Major Project 3 asked my team to design a phishing awareness toolkit for Georgia Tech students. After examining campus phishing alerts and talking with peers, we realized that many students ignore warnings because they feel repetitive or hard to act on. Our toolkit combined an educational slide deck, practical tips, and examples of real phishing messages across platforms like email, Discord, and LinkedIn. As a designer, I focused on visual hierarchy, readability, and accessibility while collaborating on content and strategy with my teammates. This project demonstrates my ability to create audience centered educational materials that fit into a larger communication ecosystem.
