Major Project 2: Equifax Crisis Poster

Analyzing a data breach as a communication failure

Project Overview

For Major Project 2, my partner and I analyzed the 2017 Equifax data breach as a technical communication crisis. Our goal was not just to describe what happened technically, but to show how Equifaxâ€™s poor consumer communication increased confusion and harm for people whose data was exposed. I led the design and visual layout of our research poster, focusing on clarity, hierarchy, and accessibility while my partner focused more on technical and cybersecurity research. We delievered the presentation together during class. This project demonstrates my ability to translate a complex incident into a visual narrative that highlights key lessons for future crisis communication.

Poster Design

The poster below tells the story of the Equifax breach through sections like background, methods, results, and recommendations. I used a clean grid, limited color palette, and visual timeline to draw attention to where consumer communication broke down and how it could be improved.
