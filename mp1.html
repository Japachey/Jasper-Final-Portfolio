Major Project 1: Job Portfolio

Professional materials for industry audiences

Project Overview

Major Project 1 asked me to build a professional job portfolio that I could share with recruiters and hiring managers. I created a resume, cover letter, and a dark themed portfolio website that highlights my background in computer science and mathematics, my research and internship experiences, and my leadership in student organizations. My goal was to make the site easy to skim while still showing depth, using clear section headings, concise impact focused bullets, and a visual layout that feels modern and professional. This project demonstrates my ability to translate a long list of experiences into a focused narrative that speaks directly to an industry audience.
